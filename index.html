How MEETO Works

Three simple steps to amazing adventures

01

Create Your Travel Profile

Tell us about your travel style, interests, and what kind of adventures you're seeking. Upload photos, set your preferences, and let our AI understand your perfect travel personality.

02

Find Perfect Travel Buddies

Browse through verified travelers in your destination. Use filters to find people who match your vibe, schedule, and interests. Our AI suggests the best matches based on compatibility scores.

03

Plan & Adventure Together

Use our collaborative planning tools to create the perfect itinerary. Chat, share locations, vote on activities, and make memories. Everything you need in one place.
